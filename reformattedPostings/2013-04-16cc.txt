Oli Scarff/Getty ImagesThe IMF has reduced its world growth forecast for 2013 to 3.3% from 3.5%, according to the agency's latest release. Practically no one was safe. Euro-area outlook was cut to -0.3% from +0.1%. U.S. growth was slashed  -0.2% to 1.9%. China was cut to 8.0% from 8.1%. Russia was cut to 3.4% from 3.7%.
