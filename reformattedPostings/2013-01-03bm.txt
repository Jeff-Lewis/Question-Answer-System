Transocean Ltd.'s stock is popping after the Wall Street Journal's Tom Fowler first reported the company agreed to pay $1.4 billion to settle claims related to 2010's Deepwater Horizon disaster. Transocean owned the Deepwater Horizon rig. Eleven workers were killed when a methane bubble shot up through the rig and combusted. The incident led to the largest offshore oil spill in U.S. history.
