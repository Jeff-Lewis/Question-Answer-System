Daniel Goodman / Business InsiderGrubhub Seamless, the restaurant delivery online and mobile app service, will file for an IPO in 2014, according to tweets from The Street and Jonathan Marino of The Deal. Seamless merged with rival GrubHub in May of this year.  Matt Maloney, GrubHub's CEO, became the new company's chief executive officer. Jonathan Zabusky, Seamless' CEO, became its president. Since then Yelp also entered the food delivery business — so this is a category that is heating up.
