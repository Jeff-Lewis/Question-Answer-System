It's not a huge move, but the market is falling during the Ben Bernanke press conference. In the immediate aftermath of the Fed statement (@ 2 PM) markets fell modestly. Now the Dow is down 138, and interest rates are spiking. Yield on the 10-year bond has hit 2.33% (!), which is the highest level since early 2012. The dollar is strengthening. What's the new news? Not a whole lot, but during the press conference Bernanke made clear that should current economic forecasts come true, then a tapering of asset purchases would be appropriate.
