ISM's February non-manufacturing survey is out. The headline index rose to 56.0 from 55.2 last month. Economists predicted that the index would moderate slightly to 55.0. The gains were led by the inventories sub-index, which rose to 54.0 from 47.0, and the backlog of orders sub-index, which rose to 54.5 from 49.0. The new export orders sub-index also posted strong gains, rising to 60.5 from 55.5, while the new orders sub-index advanced to 58.2 from 54.4 last month.
