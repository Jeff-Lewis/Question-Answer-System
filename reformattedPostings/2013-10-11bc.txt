Via Houlihan Lawrence The Wall Street Journal reports that former investment banker Joseph Zimmel and Alison Blood, a former Wall Streeter, are selling their Connecticut compound for $10.85 million.  They bought the seven-bedroom home features an indoor basketball court, an indoor lacrosse turf, heated pool/hot tub and an indoor movie theater.   The couple bought the place back in July for $10.65 million. However, they only have one child left at home since the others have gone away to school in other states, the report said.  Houlihan Lawrence in Greenwich has the listing.  * Correction: An earlier version of this story erroneously referred to Alison Blood as Joseph Zimmel's wife.
