Construction spending missed expectations falling 0.6% in June. One of the notable drags on construction was the decline in spending on religious constructions, which was down 6.8% on the month, and 12.2% on the year. A further breakdown, shows that private religious construction spending was down 7.3% on the month and 12.7% on the year.
