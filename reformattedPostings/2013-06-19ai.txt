APRio De Janiero and Sao Paolo will reduce public transit fares in response to the massive protests that met the proposal, Brazil's Globo news outlet reports . The mayor of Rio said public transport fares will be reduced to R$ 2.75 from R$ 2.95, Globo says. In São Paulo, the Governor and Mayor announced train, subway and bus fares will be $R3.00 instead of R$ 3.20. NPR's Eydar Peralta reported earlier today 11 other cities had already bowed to pressure to reduce fares.
