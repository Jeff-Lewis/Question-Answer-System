The oil and gas extraction industry added 1,100 jobs in February.  This is 27th-straight where the increase in payrolls hasn't been negative.  This is according to new stats from the Bureau of Labor Statistics.  The last time the sector saw a monthly decline was Nov. 2010.  The oil and gas drilling industry joins the Chicago Blackhawks (24) and the Miami Heat (16), two sports teams who haven't lost in a long time.
