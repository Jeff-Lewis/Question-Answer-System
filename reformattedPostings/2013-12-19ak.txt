Cigar Aficionado is out with its annual best cigars of the year. The 2013 champion? The Montecristo No. 2, the "undisputed king of torpedoes." Here's how the magazine described the winner (naturally a Cuban cigar): Recent production Monte 2s (those we smoked were from April 2013) are extraordinary, teeming with rich but not overwhelming flavors of leather, such sweet spices as cinnamon and nutmeg and the cigar’s trademark tangy wood note. They have enough flavor and power to satisfy those who smoke cigars on a regular basis without overwhelming those who puff less frequently. Puff away.
