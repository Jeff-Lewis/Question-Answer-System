Bloomberg TelevisionIndustrial machinery behemoth Caterpillar announced its Q4 2012 financial results this morning. And in its announcement were some macro economic observations. "Overall, we expect the world economy will begin the year with weak growth and improve as 2013 unfolds," they wrote. "We anticipate overall world economic growth of at least 2.5 percent—a small improvement from our estimate of 2.3 percent for 2012." They attribute some of this acceleration to global low interest rate policy by the world's central banks.
