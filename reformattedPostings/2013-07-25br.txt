Facebook's stock was up 26% shortly after today's opening bell following a glowing earnings report from the company yesterday. Most importantly, Facebook showed significant revenue growth in mobile ads, which accounted for 41% of the company's ad revenues last quarter. In Q1 2013, Facebook reported $375 million in mobile ad revenue. That figure skyrocketed to $656 million in Q2 of this year.
