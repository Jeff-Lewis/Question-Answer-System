michaelvito / FlickrTuna auction in JapanJapan's latest trade numbers are out. The trade deficit came in at ¥641.5 billion, which was wider than the ¥522.8 billion forecast by economists. Exports fell by 5.8 percent year-over-year versus the estimate of -4.2 percent. Exports to China fell 15.8 percent. Exports to the EU fell 11.1 percent. Export to the US fell 0.8 percent.
