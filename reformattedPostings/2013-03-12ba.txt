Bloomberg via YouTubeHousing experts have been raising their home price forecasts lately.  Both Bank of America and Capital Economics are calling for an 8 percent rise in 2013. In a new note, Bank of America's Michelle Meyer writes that while gains were driven by the supply side of the equation last year (i.e. declining inventory), home prices are expected to be driven by increasing demand this year.
