Flickr via USDAgovThe April reading of U.S. factory orders is out, and it's a miss. Orders climbed by just 1.0%, month-over-month in April, missing economists' forecasts for a 1.5% gain. To make things worse, the March growth number was revised down to -4.7% from -4.0%. "A 3.3% rise in the durables portion has already been reported," noted High Frequency Economics' Jim O'Sullivan.
