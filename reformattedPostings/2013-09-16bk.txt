APIndustrial production climbed 0.4% in August, slightly missing analyst expectations. Economists expected that industrial production — which includes  manufacturing, mining, and electric and gas utilities — would climb 0.5% after a 0% print in July. August purchasing managers' indices generally improved in August and employment data showed that aggregate hours worked in manufacturing also rose, Wells Fargo's John Silvia wrote to clients.
