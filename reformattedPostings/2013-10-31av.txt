A year ago this week, Hurricane Sandy devastated coastal communities from Jamaica to Canada. In the U.S. alone, the storm caused an estimated $65 billion in damages. The Tri-State area was arguably hit the hardest, and some families still haven't recovered. To show the devastation and recovery — or lack thereof — Getty photographers published a series of shots from the disaster and a year later.
