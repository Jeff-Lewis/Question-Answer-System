Wikimedia CommonsSbarro is going to close 155 stores in an attempt to become more profitable.  The pizza chain most commonly seen in mall food courts is still trying to recover from its 2011 bankruptcy, reports Lauren Coleman-Lochner at Bloomberg News.  "The move is part of a broader plan to boost financial performance under a new management team," Lochner writes.
