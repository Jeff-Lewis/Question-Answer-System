Callaway Henderson/ Sotheby's International RealtyThe Wall Street Journal reports that a former hedge fund manager is selling his Princeton, New Jersey home for $3.575 million.  Daniel Cloud is a founding partner of Firebird Fund Management.  He's currently a lecturer at Princeton University's philosophy department, according to the Journal.  Cloud purchased the Buddhism-influenced 7,000 square foot home for $3.7 million in 2007, the report said.  He told the paper he wants to sell it because it's too much for one person.
