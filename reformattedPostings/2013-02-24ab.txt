China Photos/Getty ImagesChinese Flash HSBC manufacturing PMI fell to a four-month low of 50.4. Analysts polled by Bloomberg were looking for a reading of 52.2. This is down from the final January reading of 52.3.  A reading below 50 indicates contraction. The decline in new export orders shows that weakness in the U.S. and Europe are still weighing on Chinese manufacturing.
