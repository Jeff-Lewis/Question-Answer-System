ShutterstockSeptember U.S. consumer credit data are out. Total credit expanded by $13.737 billion in September to $3.052 trillion, above consensus estimates for a $12.000 billion rise. August consumer credit growth was revised up to $14.152 billion from $13.625 billion. Revolving credit — credit cards and the like — fell $2.1 billion to $846.9 billion, marking the fourth straight month of decline.
