Wikimedia CommonsEarly this morning the New York Post reported, citing sources, that former NYC mayor Ed Koch has died at the age of 88. The Associated Press has now confirmed the report. The Post report says the former mayor passed away at 2 AM. It has been known that Koch has been in and out of intensive care in recent weeks. Koch served as mayor from 1978 to 1989.
