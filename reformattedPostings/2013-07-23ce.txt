REUTERS/Arnd WiegmannCarsten Schloter, the CEO of Switzerland's largest phone company Swisscom, was found dead in a suspected suicide, Bloomberg reports. Schloter's body was found in his Freiburg home, and police said there was a "very high" probability he killed himself, a police spokesman told Bloomberg. Swisscom is “deeply saddened and pass on their condolences to the family and relatives,” Chairman Hansueli Loosli said in the statement.
