Markets staged a major rally on Friday after the US jobs report came in way better than expected. And that rally is continuing today. Playing catch-up, Japan gained 2.29%. Italy is up 0.5%. Germany is up 0.3%. And US futures are up modestly. This week is going to be much quieter than last week in terms of economic data, although there will be a Fed decision later this week where they might "taper."
