ISPs are the least satisfying industry in America, according to the latest ratings from the American Customer Satisfaction Index (ACSI), which are based on extensive surveys. Despite service disruptions, high bills, and awful call center experiences, consumers are dependent on these services and often have few companies to choose from.  Other low-rated industries include subscription television, social media, and power companies. The biggest drop since our last ranking went to the Long Island Power Authority, broadly criticized for its response to Hurricane Sandy.   FREE AppDownload
