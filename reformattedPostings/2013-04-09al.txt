Peter Orszag, the former director of the Office of Management and Budget, is an avid runner. He used to go for his daily run at 4:30am, but now that he is the Vice Chairman at Citi, he has switched to running at 9pm instead. Orzsag, who runs about a 8:30 minute mile, goes for 4-5 mile run on a weekday, and a 7-10 mile run during the weekend. He is also a huge country music fan, having quoted Toby Keith during a Congressional hearing back in 2009.
