At Seasonal Odds we analyzed the average historical performance of Anheuser-Busch (BUD) as well as key market indices in the three trading days following the Super Bowl over the last 10 Super Bowls. BUD averages a positive return of .37% in the three days following the Super Bowl, whereas the market as a whole averages a loss over the same period.
