Goldman Sachs CEO Lloyd Blankfein was awarded $13.3 million for his stock bonus for 2012, Bloomberg News Christine Harper reports citing a regulatory filing.  That's up 90% compared to 2011, the report said. It's unclear what Blankfein's cash bonus is for 2012, but he normally gets 70% of his bonus in stock, according to the report.  He's also paid a $2 million cash salary.  The investment banking giant crushed its fourth quarter earnings results that were released this week.
