Bloomberg TVLast night, euro zone leaders finally agreed on a deal to bail out Cyprus' financial system.  Among other things, depositors with over 100,000 euros in their accounts will get slammed. While an immediate and disorderly collapse of Cyprus' banks may have been averted, this story clearly isn't over yet. In his daily comment on UBS.com, economist Paul Donovan notes that Cyprus' current financial system continues to be unsustainable.  Furthermore, he warns that the precedent set by deal regarding depositor haircuts could make depositors in other countries worried.
