REUTERS/China Here's more evidence of a medium-term recovery in China. The official PMI reading for August has risen to 51.0, vs. 50.3 last month, and 50.6 expected (via ForexLive). Chinese growth has been underwhelming this year, but the last couple of months have seen somewhat better data, which makes a "hard landing seem a bit less likely."
