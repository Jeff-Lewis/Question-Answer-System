This morning's August jobs report brought us news that, once again, labor force participation dropped. The rate fell to 63.2% from 63.4% the month before. That's the lowest since August 1978. So as the unemployment rate steadily drops, it's important to keep labor force participation — arguably a better indicator of real employment — in mind.
