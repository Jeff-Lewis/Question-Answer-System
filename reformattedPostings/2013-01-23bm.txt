eFinancial Careers, the world's leading career networking site for the financial services industry, has released their 2013 Hiring Trend Survey. For about a month (from December to January) they polled over 130 Wall Street recruiting and HR professionals from top firms around the world to get their take on this new year. In  a nutshell, Wall Streeters with 2-10 years experience in risk and tech have the best prospects. Hiring is picking up fastest in Asia and slowest here in the United States. As for salaries, expect them to stay flat or trend slightly higher. Now for the details.
