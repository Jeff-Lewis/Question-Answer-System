FordMay auto sales numbers were released today, and major manufacturers offered info on how many vehicles they sold last month. And it was good news: total vehicle sales in May were 15.24 million at a seasonally adjusted annualized rate, up from 14.88 million in April.  This was ahead of consensus estimates for 15.1 million.
