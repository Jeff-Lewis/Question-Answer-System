ISMThe results of the Institute for Supply Management's October non-manufacturing survey are out. The headline gauge rose to 55.4 from September's 54.4 reading, defying expectations for a drop to 54.0. The number suggests an unexpected acceleration in the pace of growth in the American services sector in October. The employment sub-index rose to 56.2 from September's 52.7 reading, suggesting an acceleration in the pace of hiring as well. A complete breakdown of the report's sub-indices is shown at right.
