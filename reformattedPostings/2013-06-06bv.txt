UPDATE: The latest weekly jobless claims data are out. During the week ended June 1, claims fell to 346,000 from an upward-revised 357,000 in the previous week. Economists were predicting a slightly larger drop to 345,000. Continuing claims fell to 2.952 million during the week ended May 25 from an upward-revised 3.004 million the week before.
