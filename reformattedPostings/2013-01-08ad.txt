In a slide titled "The After Math Of The Housing Bubble," JP Morgan Funds's David Kelly offers a chart of monthly rent versus monthly mortgage payments to capture an imbalance in the housing market. Americans continue to be unable or unwilling to buy a house.  Many still remember what happened in the housing bubble not just a few years ago. Meanwhile, this the demand for rentals have exploded, so much so that average rents are much higher than average mortgage payments.
