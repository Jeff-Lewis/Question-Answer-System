Julia La Roche for Business InsiderWe recently spoke to a well-connected Wall Streeter who told us the No. 1 piece of advice bankers have for new recruits. It's something that no New York summer interns are going to want to hear. Don't go to the Hamptons. Why? If you really want a job offer, then you need to spend almost every waking hour in the office that you possibly can. That also means you need to make yourself available to try new things at your firm.
