San Diego real estate god Jim Klinge points us to this map showing the three year price changes for all U.S. metros from Mortgage News Daily. On balance, more areas are growing than not. Declines are largely concentrated in the northwest and southeast.   But Klinge warns the trend will not continue: ...the strong investor demand for foreclosed properties, record levels of housing affordability and other demand factors that have driven recent double-digit price gains are unlikely to persist throughout the year.
