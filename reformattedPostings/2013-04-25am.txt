China has a major corruption problem. "2013 will mark the most vigorous anti-corruption fight by the Chinese leadership in decades," write the analysts at Deutsche Bank. This initiative is expected to be bad news for China's liquor suppliers, watch-makers, and food-service industry workers.  But it'll be a big win for investor confidence. In its new "Equity House View" report, Deutsche Bank includes this stunning chart showing how much more wealthy Chinese congressman are compared to American politicians.
