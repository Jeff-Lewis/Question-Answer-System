WikimediaGermany wants to end its virtual moratorium on fracking, Der Spiegel reports. "Parliamentarians from Angela Merkel's governing center-right coalition of the conservative Christian Democratic Union, their Bavarian sister party the Christian Social Union, and the pro-business Free Democrats (FDP) want to jumpstart the extraction of shale gas deposits in the country. As such, they have agreed on key measures for tapping virgin deposits ahead of this year's federal election in the fall." While the country doesn't have an official ban, the government has so far refused to grant permits to drill using the process over safety fears.
