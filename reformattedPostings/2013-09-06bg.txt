REUTERSThe August nonfarm payrolls report was just released, missing economist expectations. The labor force participation rate fell to 63.2% from 63.4% the month before. The American economy added 169,000 nonfarm payrolls in August. Economists were expecting 180,000 new payrolls in this report. The unemployment rate dropped to 7.3% from 7.4%, largely thanks to those leaving the workforce. The unemployment rate has been steadily dropping for some time now.
