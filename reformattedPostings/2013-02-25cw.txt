Market's are up across Europe.  Italy is doing particularly well, up 2.3 percent. This rally comes as Italians vote in their general election.  Polls close at 9:00 AM ET.  As BI's Joe Weisenthal reports, "There are worries that strong performances today by either Silvio Berlusconi or Beppe Grillo (the anti-bank populist comedian) will create chaos."
