MSVG via flickrU.S. auto sales fell 4.2% in September from the year-earlier month, Wards Auto is reporting. Total sales were at 15.34 million at a seasonally-adjusted rate, missing expectations. Analysts were expecting total sales to fall to 15.80 million units at a seasonally adjusted annualized rate, a drop from August's 16.02 million figure (Labor Day sales figured were included in August's figures, not September's).
