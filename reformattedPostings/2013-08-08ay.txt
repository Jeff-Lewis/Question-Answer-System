Tesla was up 15% to $154.81 earlier today. Tesla is now up 17.6% to $157.87. Shares surged after-hours on Wednesday after Tesla's Q2 earnings report beat expectations. The company reported Q2 profit of $0.20 per share, on revenue of $405 million. This beat expectations for a loss of $0.19 per share, on revenue of $387.9 million. Goldman Sach's Patrick Archambault has since raised his six-month price target to $95 from $84. But he thinks at today's price, the stock is expensive, even in their most optimistic scenario.
