The wait is over. The results of the December 2012 Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) Level 1 exams are out, and they are being emailed to candidates today. The good news: 37 percent passed. The bad news: 63 percent failed. The results will be available on the CFA Institute 's website after 9:00 AM ET on Wednesday January 23.
