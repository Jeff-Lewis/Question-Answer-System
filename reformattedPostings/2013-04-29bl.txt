Images_Of_Money via flickrThe March reading of pending home sales is out. Sales jumped 1.5% from last month.  Economists were looking for a 1.0% gain. However, February's reading was revised down to -1.0% from an initial reading of -0.4%. "Contract activity has been in a narrow range in recent months, not from a pause in demand but because of limited supply," said Lawrence Yun of the National Association of Realtors.  "Little movement is expected in near-term sales closings, but they should edge up modestly as the year progresses. Job additions and rising household wealth will continue to support housing demand."
