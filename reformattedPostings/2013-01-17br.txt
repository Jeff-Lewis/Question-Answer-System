BloombergTV star Stephanie Ruhle is pregnant! The former Wall Streeter and anchor of Market Makers and Lunch Money will be having a bouncing baby girl in April. "I've spent 14 years on a trading floor of boys. I've got two at home, plus my husband," Ruhle told Business Insider. "One of the best parts about coming to Bloomberg was finally being surrounded by great women and now I'll be adding another one to the mix since I'm expecting a baby girl in April."
