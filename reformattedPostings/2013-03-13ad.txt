In a new note out this evening, Citi macro strategist Jeremy Hale unveils a series of new trades, several of which can be characterized as: GO AMERICA. Here are some of the big ones, with our summary of his rationale in bullets: Sell US Treasuries Long the dollar vs. G10 currencies  Go long US equities vs. corporate credit There's no doubt that the bulls are on parade. This comes after Morgan Stanley's big call about an "inflection point" for the US economy. Heck, even Richard Russell is a bull. There's a feeling that (and this is especially so after today's retail sales report) that the US economy is bulletproof, the Fed isn't going to screw it up, and things won't be too bad in Washington, beyond what everyone expected.
