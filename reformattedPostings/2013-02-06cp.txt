According to the popular stock market January Effect, "As goes January, so goes the year." And the S&amp;P 500 soared 5 percent in January. "In sum, since 1928, when the S&amp;P 500 has risen in January, it's climbed an average of 8.3% for the next 11 months and was up over that span nearly 78% of the time," writes Charles Schwab's Liz Ann Sonders.
