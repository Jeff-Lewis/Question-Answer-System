REUTERS/Eric ThayerThe NASDAQ MarketSite in New York's Times Square.Options trading briefly froze today after the Options Price Reporting Authority (OPRA) saw a system glitch. Nasdaq, the CBOE, Bats and NYSE Euronext all ceased trading. "NASDAQ Options Market (NOM) has halted trading in a issues A thru M as of 13:35:33, N thru Z as of 13:35:47 due to OPRA system issues," Nasdaq issued in a system status message.
