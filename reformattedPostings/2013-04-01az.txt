Corn futures continued their massive sell off today as Goldman Sachs cut its price forecast a full dollar, to $6.50 per bushel from $7.50, Reuters reported. They were down as much as 8 percent Monday to $6.40, hitting lows not seen since last June.   Here's the chart: Investing.com A bearish USDA report Thursday saying corn supplies had come in higher than expected kicked off the collapse.
