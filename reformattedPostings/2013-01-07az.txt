According to an online survey of American consumers conducted by Accenture, "one-half of U.S. consumers are likely to make a small luxury purchase in the next six months." The survey found that while Millennials were more inclined to make luxury purchases than they were in the past, Baby Boomers indicated that their likelihood to purchase luxury good decreased in the past year.
