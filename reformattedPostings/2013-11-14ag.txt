David Tepper's Appaloosa Management picked up a small stake in embattled retailer JC Penney last quarter, according to filings with the SEC.  Specifically, the fund picked up 737,800 shares of JCP — the stock is up slightly in after hours trading. Appaloosa also added slightly to its stakes in JP Morgan and Citigroup.
