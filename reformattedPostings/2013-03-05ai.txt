It's just been reported that Venezuelan President Hugo Chavez is dead. What's the market reaction? Below is a chart of Venezuelan 15-year government bond yields. The chart shows that anyone hoping to profit from Chavez's death probably already missed the rally, as investors have been snapping up these bonds for nearly a year, betting on Chavez's deteriorating health.
