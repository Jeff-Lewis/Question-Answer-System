Based on inflation, unemployment, growth weakness, and cost of capital, Goldman notes that emerging market's "macro-misery" indices have pushed back to post-financial-crisis highs. It is hardly a surprise that macroeconomic hardship is surging since in the 15 years since the EM sovereign bonds have been liquid, levels remain extremely elevated, despite the mainstream-media's relegation of the problem.
