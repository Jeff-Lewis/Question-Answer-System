ISMTable 1.The results of the Institute for Supply Management's monthly survey of American services-sector firms are out. The report's headline index fell to 51.6 from January's 54.0 reading, missing economists' consensus estimate of 53.5. The closely-watched employment sub-index fell to 47.5 from January's 56.4 reading, indicative of a contraction in services-sector hiring over the past month.
