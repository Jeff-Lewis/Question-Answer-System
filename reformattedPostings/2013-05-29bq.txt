Steve Kovach/Business InsiderThe BlackBerry Q10.BlackBerry's new keyboard-equipped phone, the Q10, will launch on T-Mobile June 5 for $100 down plus $20 per month for 24 months.  This is BlackBerry's first phone with a physical keyboard that runs the company's new mobile operating system BlackBerry 10. BlackBerry 10 originally debuted on the Z10, a touchscreen-only phone. The Q10 is a great phone, but you should only buy it if you think a physical keyboard is the most important feature.
