Twitter stock had a bad day again, closing down 5% at $60.51 per share. After a massive run last week, the stock began to collapse. It fell 13% on Friday. Even during Twitter's big run, one analyst, Robert Peck of Sun Trust, decided to downgrade the stock. Peck put a $50 target on Twitter. Interestingly enough, Peck was one of the first analysts to issue a buy rating for Twitter before the company had its IPO.
