Flickr via Kheel Center, Cornell UniversityProducer prices rose +0.7 percent.  Core prices gained +0.2 percent. That's basically in line with expectations. Non-core was expected at +0.6 percent, core at +0.2 percent. Both came in at +0.2 percent last month. HERE'S THE FULL RELEASE FROM THE BLS &gt; On an unadjusted basis, the finished goods index moved up 1.7 percent YOY, the largest 12-month increase since a 2.3-percent rise in October 2012. The index for intermediate materials, supplies, and components moved up 1.3  percent in February, the largest increase since a 1.4-percent jump in April 2011.
