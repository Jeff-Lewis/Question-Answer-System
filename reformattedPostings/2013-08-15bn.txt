AP Photo/ Louis LanzanoConsumer prices climbed 0.2% month-over-month (MoM) in July, in line with expectations. Core CPI (ex-food and energy) also climbed 0.2% in line with expectations. Consumer prices were up 2% on the year and core CPI was up 1.7% on the year, in line with expectations.  Investors watch this number because it is a monthly indicator of inflation and gives us insight into the cost of living.
