The Fed has been trying to boost employment by keeping interest rates way low. The theory is, companies will be more willing to borrow and invest in their businesses if banks are giving them good opportunities to do so. The Fed has acknowledged its easing policy has not come without costs, especially for savers. But on the surface, inflation has remained stable. It's not quite so simple, however.
