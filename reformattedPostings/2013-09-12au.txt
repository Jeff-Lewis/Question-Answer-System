Wikimedia Commons/Florian HirzingerThe U.S. government posted a deficit of $147.9 billion in August, according to the latest monthly budget statement just released by the Treasury Department. Economists expected the Treasury to report a slightly smaller August deficit of $146 billion. Government spending outlays were $333 billion, while receipts were $185 billion. This month's deficit is down 22% from last year's August deficit of $190.5 billion. In July, the Treasury posted a deficit of $97.6 billion. FREE AppDownload
