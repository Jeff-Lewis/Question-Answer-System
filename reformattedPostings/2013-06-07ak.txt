Flickr / iujazUPDATE: April consumer credit data are out. Total credit expanded $11.06 billion – or 4.7% from a year ago – in April to $2.82 trillion after rising an upward-revised $8.37 billion in March. Economists were looking for a $12.9 billion advance. Revolving credit – credit cards and the like – rose $700 million, or 1.0% from a year ago, to $849.8 billion.
