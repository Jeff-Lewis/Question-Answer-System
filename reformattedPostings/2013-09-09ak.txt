Total credit rose a seasonally-adjusted $10.437 billion in July (4.4% year over year) to $2.852 trillion, below estimates for a larger $12.700 billion expansion. June's credit expansion was revised down to $11.925 billion (a 5.1% year-over-year rise) from the initially-reported $13.818 billion figure. Revolving credit – credit cards and the like – fell $1.8 billion, or 2.6% year over year, to $849.8 billion.
