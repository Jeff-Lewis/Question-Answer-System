REUTERS/Sean YongJuly auto sales are in, and major manufacturers posted major gains despite Detroit's "Big Three" falling below analysts' expectations. Ford sales climbed 11% from the month a year ago, with a 22.6% increase in the F-Series pickup. Total U.S. new vehicle sales slipped to a seasonally adjusted annual rate of 15.6 million.
