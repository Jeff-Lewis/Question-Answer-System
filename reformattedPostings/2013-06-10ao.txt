Apple has redesigned the look and feel of its mobile operating system for iPhones and iPads, iOS. The new version uses a simpler "flat" design, without the fancy three-dimensionality of the old one. And as Apple had a lot of fun pointing out in its conference presentation, some of the old skeumorphic (physical-world-like) icon designs have been replaced by modern digital ones.
