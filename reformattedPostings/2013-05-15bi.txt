Today's report on industrial output was highly underwhelming. But as AEI's Mark Perry points out, there was a noticeable bright spot: oil and gas production, which jumped 9% in April and is now at levels not seen since 1972. The ongoing energy boom reflected in today’s Federal Reserve report provides further confirmation that energy remains the strongest sector of the US economy.
