After collapsing in the wake of the big protests, the Turkish stock market is crashing again today. The culprit: Comments from Prime Minister Erdogan about how development plans (plans to get rid of a park and replace it with a mall) will go ahead. This was the original spark that lit the protest fuse (although the protests are much broader). The Borsta Istanbul is now off about 6%. Via Bloomberg: Bloomberg.com FREE AppDownload
