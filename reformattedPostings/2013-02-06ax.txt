AP ImagesSixteen year-old actress Rachel Fox, who is known for playing Kayla Scavo on the hit series "Desperate Housewives," has taken up day trading when she's not on the big screen. She says that she's really good at it, too. Fox, who has a blog and video episodes called "Fox On Stocks", says that she earns more than 64 percent on her investments per year.   We reached out to Fox to find out more about her stock trading career.
