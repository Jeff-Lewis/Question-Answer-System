A newborn delivered by emergency cesarean section after his parents were killed in a horrific crash in Brooklyn has died, CBS News reports.  The boy weighed only four pounds when he was delivered early Sunday morning after his parents, Nachman and Raizy Glauber, both 21, were killed when a BMW crashed into the cab they were in. Their funeral was held on Sunday. Raizy Glauber was seven months pregnant, and her son had been in critical condition since his birth.  Police do not have anyone in custody.
