Heidi Gutman/ CNBCDaniel S. Loeb, the founder of Third Point LLC, has a reputation for his strongly worded (and public) letters to companies and CEOs. The sharp-tongued fund manager has made headlines again after taking a massive long position in Herbalife, the stock rival Bill Ackman has been publicly shorting. Third Point has disclosed that it owns 8,900,000 shares or an 8.24% stake. Ackman believes Herbalife, a multi-level marketing company that sells nutrition products, is a pyramid scheme.  Loeb doesn't believe Ackman's accusation has merit.
