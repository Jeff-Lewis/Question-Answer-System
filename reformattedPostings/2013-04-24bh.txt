REUTERS/Robert GalbraithDurable goods orders data are out. Total orders fell 5.7% in March versus expectations of a smaller, 3.0% decline. Orders for nondefense capital goods excluding aircraft and parts (a.k.a. "core capex") rose 0.2%. Economists were looking for a 0.3% increase.
