According to this map created by the Institute for Children, Poverty and Homelessness (ICPH), Manhattan's most educated live below 125th, and prefer the West side. In contrast, most people who live in uptown community districts like East Harlem and Washington Heights haven't graduated from high school. This map was created using U.S. Census Bureau, 2006–10 American Community Survey 5-year Estimates.  Check it out: Institute for Children, Poverty and Homelessness
