Reuters/China DailyOfficial Chinese manufacturing PMI climbed to 50.8 in May. A reading below 50 indicates contraction. This beat expectations for a fall to 50, and was up from April's reading of 50.6 The rise was driven by output which climbed to 53.3 in May, from 52.6 the previous month.
