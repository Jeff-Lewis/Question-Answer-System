Paul EhrlichPrivate equity investor David Valaer is selling his gorgeous contemporary Washington farmhouse for $2.9 million, the Wall Street Journal reports. The 5,600 square foot house sits on 72-acres of land and overlooks the Columbia River Gorge — a designated U.S. National Scenic Area. The house offers 5 bedrooms, 7 bathrooms, an 8 car garage, separate guest quarters, a barn with 4 stables, an airplane hanger and an FAA approved runway. The house is listed last with Joseph Brazen and Erin Riordan of Brazen Sotheby's International Realty and Steve Studley from The Hasson Company.
