There are sure are a lot of people who think natural gas prices are going to continue to go up. According to Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) data from Citi's Timothy Evans, the number of net long positions for nat gas contracts now stands at 530,786.  That's a record high. Here's the chart: Citi Natural gas prices were trading up +0.6% Friday to hit $4.05.
