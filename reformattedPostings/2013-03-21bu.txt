The latest weekly jobless claims report is out. Initial claims rose slightly to 336,000 from an upwardly-revised 334,000 the week before. However, economists expected a slightly larger rise to 340,000. Continuing claims rose to 3.053 million from an upwardly-revised 3.048 million the week before. Economists had expected continuing claims to rise to 3.050 million.
