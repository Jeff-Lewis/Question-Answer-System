AP ImagesDespite recent hiccups in gold prices, if you're investing in commodities, it is still the best, safest bet, according to Morgan Stanley's commodities team led by Hussein Allidina. Silver offers a little more risk and reward than gold; Allidina expects it to outperform gold in 2013. The report also favors soybeans and corn as demand for them is accelerating faster than supply. However, the report warns commodities investors away from aluminum and sugar, two commodities that are acutely oversupplied at the moment.
