flickr / Mike KalasnikDays after Ron Johnson was ousted as CEO at JC Penney, three top executives have reportedly headed for the exits. The New York Post's James Covert reports: As first reported by The Post, Chief Operating Officer Mike Kramer, Chief Talent Officer Daniel Walker and Chief Creative Officer Mike Fisher kicked off an exodus of high-profile Johnson hires that is expected to continue in the wake of his ouster, sources said.
