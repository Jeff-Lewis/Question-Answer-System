In the wake of the ADP private-sector payroll creation release at 8:30 AM, S&amp;P 500 futures are sinking and bonds are surging. ADP's report estimated that 166,000 private-sector payrolls were created in September – below consensus estimates for 180,000, and August's 176,000 reading was revised down to 159,000. S&amp;P 500 futures are trading down 0.7% at levels around 1677, versus levels around 1681 prior to the release. Meanwhile, 10-year U.S. Treasury futures are up 0.4%.
