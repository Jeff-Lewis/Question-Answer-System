Oil prices spent most of the day climbing steadily. But then in the last few minutes of the NYMEX session, prices tanked. The October futures contract ended closing down 1.2% for the day. One of our oil trader contacts says there was no obvious trigger, but that it was likely to have been someone with a large position "fading" the market on news that the UK seems reluctant to join in any Syria strike.
