REUTERS/Mike BlakeMortgage applications fell 1.8% in the week ending Nov. 9. This compares with -2.8% the previous week, which was revised up from an earlier estimate of -7%.  The purchase index fell 1%, down from a 5% fall the previous week. Meanwhile, the refinance index fell 2%, down from an 8% fall the previous week. The 30-year fixed mortgage rate stood at 4.16%, in the week ending Nov. 7. This is down from a recent two-year high of 4.58%. Rising mortgage rates have started to trickle into housing data.
