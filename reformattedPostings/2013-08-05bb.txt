ISMISM's monthly non-manufacturing report is out. The headline index rose to 56.0 in July from 52.2 in June, coming in well above the consensus estimate of 53.1. The business activity/production sub-index surged to 60.4 in July from 51.7 in June. The new orders index advanced to 57.7 from 50.8. The employment sub-index fell to 53.2 from 54.7, suggesting a robust but slowing pace of hiring. At right is a breakdown of the various sub-indices contained in the report.
