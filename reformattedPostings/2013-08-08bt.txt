bytemarks / FlickrThe Department of Labor's latest weekly reading of weekly initial unemployment claims is out, and it's basically in line with expectations. Claims climbed to 333,000, a level which was slightly lower than the 335,000 expected by economists. Last week's reading was revised up to 328,000 from an earlier reading of 326,000.
