UK labor statistics are out and they're encouraging for the region. The unemployment rate for the period from June to August was 7.7%, which was in line with economists' forecasts. However, those filing for unemployment claims in September plunged by 41,700, this was a sharper decline than the 25,000 expected. According to Bloomberg, this is the biggest drop since June 1997.
