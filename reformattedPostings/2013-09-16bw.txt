Flickr / LarryChicken MakhaniWholesale-prices jumped 6.1% year-over-year in August, accelerating from 5.79% in  July. Economists were looking for prices to decelerate to 5.7%. All of this comes as India's rupee, while up this month, continues to be one of the world's worst performing currencies this year. Food inflation surged 18.1%, the fastest pace in three years. "Late planting and disruptions in supplies of vegetables and onions due to heavy summer rains have fueled food inflation," reported Reuters' Rajesh Kumar Singh.
