Google FinanceThe U.S. stock markets spent most of the morning pretty deep in the red. But they've erased all of those losses and just turned positive. This contradicted the idea that the government shutdown was bad for the economy. "Economic conditions continue to improve slowly in spite of government policy," read one of the ISM's October anecdotes. At its lowest point of the day, the Dow was down by 109 points.
