AP ImagesAnd Wall Street just became even more competitive... Business Insider has just learned that Cantor Fitzgerald has hired U.S. Olympic swimmer Klete Keller. He started working in their Memphis, Tennessee offices this month, according to FINRA records. We're told he's trading debt for the firm.  Keller, 30, who has won five Olympic medals (two golds, two bronze and one silver), is best known for holding off Ian Thorpe (a.k.a "the Thorpedo") in the anchor leg of the 4x200 relay at the 2000 Olympics.
