It's Managing Director Day at Goldman Sachs!  The bank has tapped 280 people to be MDs, which is higher than last year's 266, Bloomberg pointed out.   It's a big deal to be named a managing director. The title is just one step below "partner."  The next class of MDs won't be selected until 2015.  Earlier this year, Goldman changed its managing director selection process from every year to every two years.
