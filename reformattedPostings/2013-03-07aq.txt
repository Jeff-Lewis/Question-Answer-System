Philip Taylor PTThe Federal Reserve's January G.19 report on consumer credit is out. Total consumer credit outstanding jumped by $16.15 billion, which was ahead of expectations for growth of $14.7 billion. Revolving debt (e.g. credit cards) climbed by just 0.1 percent, while non-revolving debt (e.g. car loans) jumped 10.0 percent.
