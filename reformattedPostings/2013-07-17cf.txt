lewisha1990 on flickrBank of America's Q2 financial results are out. Earnings jumped to $0.32 per share from $0.19 per share a year ago. This was well ahead of the $0.24 expected by economists. This comes on $22.9 billion of revenue. "At the beginning of the year, we said we would focus on three things revenue stability, strengthening the balance sheet and managing costs," said CFO Bruce Thompson. "This quarter, we delivered on all three.
