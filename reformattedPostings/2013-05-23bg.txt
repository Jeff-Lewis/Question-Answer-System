Last weekend, internet pioneer Ted Nelson speculated that the creator of Bitcoin (who goes by the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto) was actually Japanese math professor Shinichi Mochizuki. This was the latest theory in an extremely long hunt by various researchers and journalists trying to identify Nakamoto. Hilary Sargent at Chartgirl has put together a monster chart detailing all of the endeavors to find Nakomoto, and the various conjecturs that have been put forth about them.
