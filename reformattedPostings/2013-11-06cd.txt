Christie'sThe New York art auction season got off to a slow start last night in another blow to embattled hedge funder Steve Cohen's week. "Mann und Frau (Umarmung)" by Schiele, widely rumored to be owned by the billionaire, did not receive a single bid at Christie's auction, the FT's Elizabeth Paton reports. It has been a rough few days for Cohen, whose SAC Capital Advisors pleaded guilty to insider trading and swallowed a record $1.2 billion fine. A quarter of the "high-profile Impressionist and Modern paintings" went unsold at the auction.
