ReutersChinese consumer prices climbed 2.6% year-over-year (YoY) in August. This was in line with economists expectations. This compares with a 2.7% YoY rise in July. Meanwhile, producer prices fell 1.6%. This beat expectations for a 1.7% decline. This also compares with a 2.3% fall the previous month. PPI has been down for five straight months.
