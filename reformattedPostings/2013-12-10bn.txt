Faena Miami Beach Lloyd Blankfein and private equity tycoon Leon Black are among those said to have purchased a spot in a new $1 billion Miami luxury condominium development, Bloomberg Businessweek's Katya Kazakina reports.   Faena Miami Beach is an 18-story ocean condominium featuring 47 residences ranging from $3 million to $50 million, the report said. The development won't be complete until December 2014. It looks like it's a hot spot.
