REUTERS/Kevin Lamarque UPDATE: The May Chicago PMI survey results are out. The headline index surged to 58.7 from 49.0 last month. Economists were expecting a much smaller increase to 50.0. The number indicates that regional manufacturing in the Midwest expanded at a moderate pace in May after contracting slightly in April. The production sub-component rose to 62.7 from 49.9 in April, while the new orders index advanced to 58.1 from 53.2. The employment sub-component rose to 56.9 from 48.7.
